Electrical Contracting

With emphasis in energy, electrical, procurement and real estate, ER SYSTEMS has set out to seek, receive and meet clients' objectives. Its focus is on business development in the power, oil & gas, manufacturing and public sectors with responsibility in the provision of critical infrastructure solutions. Our goal is to ensure we impact our professionalism appropriately in all sectors by leveraging modern technologies and best practices.

We consider ourselves a viable investment partner and a responsible corporate citizen, not only to our clients but also to the various communities we engage with. Our overall disposition shall remain flexible to enable us respond timely and positively to the demands of the current business environment.
