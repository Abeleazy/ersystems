About Us

Founded on principles of integrity and excellence

ER SYSTEMS LTD is a Nigerian-based company with its head office in Lagos, Nigeria. The company is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) under the Companies and Allied Matters Act of 2020 of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. ER SYSTEMS is a private limited liability company strategically positioned to carry out business activities in energy solutions, electrical contracting, procurement and supply chain services, and real estate development.

ER SYSTEMS is a dynamic company committed to excellence in the provision of cost-effective services to corporate organizations, multinational companies, government agencies and parastatals in Nigeria. The company is goal-oriented with clients' objectives, safety and cost-effectiveness foremost in its daily operations to ensure purposeful, vibrant, technical efficiency, versatility and responsiveness to economic dictates.
